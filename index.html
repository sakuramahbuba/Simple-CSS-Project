Simple Omlette Recipe

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes or less
  • preparation: Around 5 minutes
  • Cooking: less than 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2/3 large Eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to spice
  • 1 tablespoon butter or Oil
  • Optional: fillings like cheese,ham,chicken or vegetables

Instructions

  1. Beet the Eggs:In a bowl,beet the eggs with pinch of saltand pepper until they are well mixed.You could add a tablespoon of water or milk if needed

  2. Heat the Pan:Place a non stick pan over a medium heat
  3. Cook the Omlette:Then, over medium-high heat, sauté the vegetables and garlic in olive oil until soft, about 3-5 minutes depending on the vegetables you choose.

  4. Add fillings(optional):Once the fillings are ready, it’s time to cook the omelette.Begin by whisking the eggs vigorously until they are homogenous (uniform throughout).

  5. Fold and Serve:This step is incredibly important, you want to beat those eggs until there are no pieces of whites separated from the yolks.

  6. Enjoy:Then, add the whisked eggs and swirl them in the pan until they are evenly distributed
